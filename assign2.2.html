Welcome to the responsive layout demo! This section represents the main content of the page. Resize your browser window to see how the layout changes across mobile, tablet, and desktop.

On mobile devices, the sidebar and content stack vertically. On tablets, the sidebar stretches across the top, followed by the content. On desktops, the sidebar sits neatly on the left while the content fills the right side.

This design uses Flexbox for modern responsiveness, float + clear for fallback, and media queries to adapt the layout. You can expand this template into a full website by adding more sections, images, and interactive elements.
